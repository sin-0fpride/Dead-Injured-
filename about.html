How to Play

Objective

Guess a 4-digit number within the fewest attempts.

Rules

  • If a digit is correct and in the right position → Dead (D)
  • If a digit is correct but in the wrong position → Injured (I)
  • Keep guessing until you get "4D 0I" to win!

Example

Secret number: 1589
Your guess: 1245
Result: 1D 1I (1 is Dead, 5 is Injured)

Keep guessing wisely!
